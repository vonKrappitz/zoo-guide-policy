## English

**Last updated:** February 2026

**1. Introduction**
The creator of the **Zoo Guide** app respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

**2. Data Collection**
The **Zoo Guide app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers**. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email, or log in.

**3. Locally Stored Data (Local Storage)**
For the app to function correctly, we save certain data exclusively on your device's memory. This includes:
* Selected language and preferred Zoo.
* Consent status for using the app (age confirmation).
* Locally set alarms (e.g., feeding or parking reminders).

This data never leaves your phone and can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data in your system settings.

**4. Device Permissions**
The app uses local system notifications to remind you of upcoming animal feedings or the end of your parking time. We do not track your location (GPS).

**5. Changes to the Privacy Policy**
We reserve the right to update this policy. Any changes will be posted on this page.

**6. Contact**
If you have any questions, contact us at: [kontakt@4law.online](mailto:kontakt@4law.online)
